Types of Fabrics

Cotton

Known as the most popular material in the world, cotton is a light, soft natural fabric. The fluffy fiber is extracted from the seeds of the cotton plant in a process called ginning.

Leather

Leather is a strong, flexible and durable material obtained from the tanning, or chemical treatment, of animal skins and hides to prevent decay..

Silk

The fabric has a shimmering appearance, though has interrupting patterns of weave due to its natural fibre.
