About Us

Palette is a top full-service, luxury interior design firm that has a reputation for perfection and impeccable service.

Our comprehensive design process begins with a thorough understanding of your vision,

preferences for colors, patterns and textures as well as a review of collected inspirations.

Once that is complete, our team handles every single detail of your project

to create a cohesive environment that is unique to you.

We’ll discuss your personal taste and style, your desires for the space and your lifestyle.

And then we’ll create something extraordinary.

Our clients are our top priority. Whether it’s a small space or full remodel –

we strive to create a unique vision for your space and bring it to life!
